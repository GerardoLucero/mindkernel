# Daily Focus

Read `kernel/state.md`.

Ask only one question: "How is your energy right now — high, medium, or low?"

Then respond with exactly this format, nothing more:

```
FOCUS: [one specific task from this week's top priority]
WHY: [one sentence connecting it to your stated priorities]
IF ENERGY IS LOW: [a smaller version of the same task, or a maintenance task]
```

Do not ask follow-up questions. Do not give options. Give one answer.
